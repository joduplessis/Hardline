    Welcome to Avalanche Hardline.

    We know how frustrating it is to find contact details or open massive mail clients just to quickly follow up on that project. It's why we made Hardline: to make getting hold of us, as easy as opening an app on your smartphone.

    Let us run you through the process.

    Add a new message

    On the bottom left side of the screen you'll see the "Add" button. Click it, fill in the message form and click "Create Message". It's that easy.

    Managing messages

    This is where it gets nifty. Click on "Manage" (next to the "Add" button). There will be a list of all the messages you've sent us. To the right you'll see a small number - this will indicate how many comments each message has. Click on a message to view it.

    Viewing messages

    You'll now see a nice big overview of your message. You'll also see a space where you can leave comments for us - we can then also leave comments for you on that same message. Don't worry, ours will look slighty darker so that you can easily see our replies.

    You'll be able to then mark the message as solved by clicking the "Mark as solved" button at the top. This will remove the message from the list so that your phone doesn't get cluttered with old queries.

    Settings

    Any personal & contact details you can save by clicking on the "Settings" button and filling out the form. It will help us get a hold of you if we need to. If you prefer not to have those details - we're okay with that too. :)

    Alphas & Betas.

    Hardline if new, so we're busy improving a lot of the app to make it even better and more effective.
